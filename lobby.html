Server Sunset is fun head-to-head card game about crashing servers and exploiting programs... As a system administrator you must install programs, execute tasks, and most importantly protect your three servers from being crashed by the opponent - or you lose! Each turn you will get a certain amount of memory to work with, which is your primary resource to hack a path to victory by installing programs or starting tasks. Programs are useful cards that provide subroutines and functionality to hamper your opponent or help your network. All cards are drawn from the same shared deck so you can jump in and start learning the cards easily. Cards can be either installed OR recycled for an immediate free effect. Smart management of your memory and tools will lead you to victory!
  • Carlo Guglielmin (online adaptation)
  • Based on Radlands by Daniel Piechnick
  • All Art is AI generated (boo, hiss! but fitting to the theme)
